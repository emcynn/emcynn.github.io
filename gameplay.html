Chapter 1

THE SHED

In the Shed of "It Takes Two," players take on the roles of Cody and May, who have been transformed into dolls. The chapter begins with them trying to find and reinsert missing fuses to power a machine. This involves navigating through various obstacles like jumping across platforms, avoiding hazards, and using cooperation to solve puzzles. The chapter culminates in a boss fight against an antagonistic vacuum cleaner, where one player sucks up explosives while the other directs them at the vacuum to deal damage. This chapter emphasizes teamwork, with each player performing distinct tasks that contribute to their progress and eventual victory over the boss​.

Chapter 2

THE TREE

In this chapter, Cody and May venture into a tree in their backyard and get captured by squirrels who give them weapons. Cody receives a sap gun and May gets a gun to ignite the sap. Together, they fight off wasps and solve puzzles to progress. They face several challenges, including swinging across branches, defeating wasp bosses, and navigating various obstacles. The chapter culminates in a boss fight against the Wasp Queen and an escape from the treehouse​.

Chapter 3

ROSE'S ROOM

Rose's Room of "It Takes Two," players navigate through various imaginative environments within Rose’s room. The chapter begins in a pillow fort where players encounter the first minigame, Tank Brothers. As they progress, they explore different themed areas including a space-themed section where May can walk upside down and Cody can shrink. Players solve puzzles, engage in more minigames, and interact with whimsical elements like a constellation globe and action figures from the developers' previous game. The chapter culminates with encounters involving Moon Baboon, leading into further adventures within Rose's room​​.

Chapter 4

CUCKOO CLOCK

In Chapter 4: Cuckoo Clock, Cody and May must solve intricate puzzles to progress through the clock tower. They start by tackling two towers, each requiring precise teamwork to overcome time-manipulation puzzles and platforming challenges. In the left tower, they coordinate to hit buttons in a specific order and then use fireflies to destroy it. The right tower involves manipulating time to cross platforms and reach the top. Once both towers are conquered, they face a series of puzzles inside the clock tower, including moving walls and obtaining keys to unlock gates. The chapter culminates in a boss fight against a mechanical bull, where Cody and May must use their abilities strategically to defeat it and progress to the next area​​.

Chapter 5

SNOW GLOBE

In Chapter 5: Snow Globe of "It Takes Two," players find themselves transported into a whimsical snow globe world, where they must navigate icy landscapes and overcome frosty challenges. Cody and May encounter unique puzzles and obstacles, such as manipulating snow and ice to progress through the environment. The snow globe serves as a fantastical backdrop for the couple's journey, symbolizing the coldness and isolation they feel in their strained relationship. Despite the chilly surroundings, Cody and May must work together to thaw their communication barriers and find warmth amidst the wintry landscape, ultimately bringing them closer together as they continue their adventure.

Chapter 6

THE GARDEN

Chapter 6 "It Takes Two," players venture into a lush and vibrant garden environment, teeming with life and puzzles to solve. Cody and May must navigate through the various obstacles and challenges, utilizing their unique abilities to progress. They encounter playful creatures and formidable foes, all while unraveling the deeper layers of their relationship. The garden setting serves as a metaphor for growth and renewal, as the couple learns to communicate and cooperate better amidst the beauty and challenges of nature. Through teamwork and perseverance, Cody and May continue their journey towards reconciliation and understanding.

Chapter 7

THE ATTIC

The Attic of the game "It Takes Two," players navigate through a mysterious attic filled with nostalgic items from Cody and May's past. They encounter various puzzles and challenges, including manipulating time to solve environmental puzzles, avoiding dangerous obstacles, and using teamwork to progress. The attic serves as a metaphor for the couple's relationship, with its ups and downs symbolized by the obstacles they face. Throughout the chapter, Cody and May must confront their past and work together to overcome their differences, ultimately strengthening their bond and moving forward in their journey.
